  The KIDIRA Arms Up Swaddle is designed with advanced moisture-wicking technology, ensuring your newborn stays dry and comfortable throughout the night. The inner layer features a honeycomb-shaped water-guiding channel that quickly absorbs and transfers moisture to the outer layer. This innovative design allows babies to move freely and find their most comforting position, promoting peaceful sleep and reducing the startle reflex. With its snug fit and secure closure, the swaddle keeps your baby safe and protected while maintaining a breathable environment for their delicate skin.
  Ideal for newborns aged 1-3 months and weighing 5-13lbs, KIDIRA's swaddle is perfect for both nighttime sleep and daytime naps. The arms up design accommodates natural movement, allowing babies to self-soothe without being restricted. The foot buttons make it easy to adjust for different leg lengths while preventing overheating and keeping your baby's face unmarked. This swaddle is a safer alternative to loose blankets and offers a secure, cozy, and peaceful sleep experience for your little one.
