  Biloban baby swaddles are thoughtfully crafted to offer your little one the ultimate comfort and safety. Made from ultra-soft cotton, these swaddles ensure a breathable, stretchy, and cozy wrap for your newborn. Each swaddle is designed with adjustable hook-and-loop tabs that allow you to secure it perfectly around your baby’s chest, adapting to their growth and development. With these swaddles, you can help your baby sleep through the night and reduce night waking caused by startle reflex.
